Welcome to the World of Calisthenics

Calisthenics is a form of exercise that focuses on using your own body weight to increase your strength and fitness levels. This website aims to inform you on the benefits of calisthenics, share my personal experiences, and provide resources to help you start your journey towards better mental and physical health.
