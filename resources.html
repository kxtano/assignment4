Affordable tools for Calisthenics

Resistance Bands

Resistance bands are versatile tools that can increase your strength and flexibility. They are great for adding resistance to bodyweight exercises and can be used for many workouts.

Buy Some Resistance Bands

Pull-Up Bar

A pull-up bar is a great piece of equipment for your upper body strength. It allows you to perform pull-ups, chin-ups, and other exercises that target the back, arms, and shoulders. Honestly, this is my personal favorite and this is what I use the most, mainly because I use it to do different pushups and pull-ups. The pull-up bar is tied in terms of versatility with resistance bands.

Buy A Pull-Up Bar

By the way, these two are the most affordable options for someone who wants to get in shape and is just starting out, this is all I use and I can say that it works just fine.
